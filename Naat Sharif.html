What is Naat And where to get it form

Naat is Islamic poetry to praise and honor Prophet Muhammad, may peace be upon him. Due to the respect in the content Naats are some sometimes called Naat e Sherif, Naat e Mubarak , Naat e Pak etc. All these roughly translates to Noble Naat, Holy Naat Nasheed and Naats go hand in hand. While in the strict sense Naat is in the honor of the Prophet ( SAWS), Nasheeds ( there are other variations such as Nasyid) are singing based on Islam. It will cover a wide spectrum of Islamic teachings, history etc. Sometimes Nasheed is used refer to Naat. Naats are obviously very popular in Islamic countries. However, Urdu and Arabic Naats are even popular.

Origin of Naat
Even during the life time of the Prophet (SAWS), his companions would love to praise his honor. This had even gone to the level that Prophet himself had asked his beloved companions to follow moderation in praising him. The origin of Naat should be from that time. Even though it is very difficult to trace back the authenticity, many of the companions are believed to have come up with their own Naats. SufiSaints have a list of companions ( Sahaba ) who have compiled Naats.

Where to get it?
Youtube will give you a good collection of Naats and there are many web sites that provide references to good Naat collection.
Is Naat worship?
Worship of anyone besides Almighty is strictly prohibited in Islam. Naat is never worship. Worship involves love and respect. But love and respect do not always mean worship. Naat is meant for the sole purpose of love and respect of the Prophet. It helps believer develop a loving connection with the Prophet. Naats in ImanSearch
